Questions and Solutions
We suggest that you print the exam questions and try to solve them before you look at the solutions.
If you have any questions about the exam, the exam questions or the exam solutions, contact Arup Guha or
one of the course instructors.
A good understanding of the concepts and problems presented in this exam is a strong indication that you are prepared
for upcoming exams.
This exam is posted to provide an example of a recent exam.  Posting this exam does not imply that future exams will
contain the same questions or follow the same format.  However, the format used in this exam is typical of the most
recent exams given and there are no immediate plans to make major changes to the exam format.  As expected, the
Department of Computer Science reserves the right to modify the exam format if, in the opinion of the faculty, such a change
is warranted.
